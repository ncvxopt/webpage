Zepeng Zhang now is a PhD student at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). Previously, he received his master's degree at ShanghaiTech University and B.Eng. degree from Wuhan University (WHU). He also had the opportunity to visit Peking University (PKU) and City University of HongKong (CityU) as a research intern. His current research focuses on graph machine learning and algorithmic reasoning. To get more recent info about him: zepengzhang.com.

Zepeng's research in DSAI lab has been mainly dedicated to two fields, namely, an optimization perspective for understanding/designing graph neural networks and non-convex optimization for weighted sum-rate maximization problems in wireless communications.
